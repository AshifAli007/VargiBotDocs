Problems with current Warehouse Systems

1. Inaccurate Inventory

Incomplete or inaccurate records often reveal themselves when a warehouse worker attempts to retrieve product from an expected location and it is not there or then they are directed to store product into a location that’s already full. Time and effort are wasted making physical checks and trying to correct error, often leading to delayed shipments and mis-picks.

2. Redundant Activities

The existence of unnecessary, excessive handling of product plagues many warehouses, with cases or pallets being moved through several intermediate locations. Often, though, redundant activities can be less obvious. Duplication of information and duplicate data entry are examples of major timewasters.

3. Poor Layout/Space Utilization

Achieving good space utilization has taken on new urgency with the rise of e-commerce. In fulfillment centers, there’s more movement and picking, increasing the cost of poor layout. Also, warehouse locations close to population centers often occupy more expensive land, which drives up costs.

4. High Labor Costs

Managers at distribution and fulfillment centers are under increasing pressure to reduce costs while improving service levels.Picking is still, for now, a highly manual task, although solutions continue to emerge. Consider how technology can increase pick rates and how pallet flow systems and AS/RS technology should be part of the solution

How Our Project Works

The main focus of our project is to automate the operations of warehouse such as picking and sorting of the packages based on their priority.

Online Order Placement

As it is based on iot so the required package/order can be ordered online and the warehouse system is also connected using IoT so as soon as an order is placed the managment sytems become active and starts the processing.

The records of order are saved in spread sheet and also a dashboard is used to see all the processes of warehouse systems in real time.

Warehouse System

With the use of IoT Warehouse system is capable of recieving the orders online so as soon as a package/order is recieved by this warehouse it checks the Inventory if the order quantity can be shipped or not and than it activates both the ur5 arms to complete the shipping process of desired package. As soon as the packages are dispatched or shipped this system is updating the order status online in a spreadesheet and user interactive Dashboard in real time. Both the ur5 arm uses intelligent sensors to detect the pacakges and sort them for shipping. It is aslo capable of taking multiple orders at a time but will process the higher priority packgages first. Thus reducing the time for the high priority orders to ship.
